Glorious Hobbies

Jules has a fascinating array of hobbies that keep her life vibrant and full of adventure. She is an avid reader, often losing herself in the pages of a good book, which provides both an escape and a source of knowledge. Her love for true crime leads her to binge-watch murder documentaries, unraveling the mysteries and complexities of criminal minds. Recently, she's been learning to rollerblade, embracing the thrill and challenge of mastering this fun activity. She enjoys the unique experience of sitting on the beach in her rain boots, savoring the tranquility and beauty of the ocean.

Jules is also a nature enthusiast, often going for hikes to explore the great outdoors and enjoy the fresh air. Camping is another passion, though she prefers the more comfortable version known as glamping. She's adventurous when it comes to food, always eager to try new restaurants or sample unusual cuisines. The Iowa State Fair is a highlight of her year, where she indulges in the festivities and the diverse culinary delights. These hobbies reflect Jules' adventurous spirit and her zest for life, always seeking new experiences and enjoying the simple pleasures along the way.
